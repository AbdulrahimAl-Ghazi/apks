Privacy Policy for WallpaperCreator

Effective Date: July 21, 2025

1. Data We Do Not Collect Directly

WallpaperCreator does not directly collect personal information such as: name, email, phone number, photos, or precise location. The app does not require users to sign in or share any identifiable data.

2. Ads and Third-Party Services

The app displays ads through third-party networks like Google AdMob. These providers may collect limited non-personal data such as:

  • Device type and operating system
  • Approximate location (e.g. country or region)
  • Ad interaction data (e.g. views, clicks)

This data is used for ad targeting and performance measurement. We do not access or store this data ourselves. Users are encouraged to review the privacy policies of these third-party services.

3. Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Storage access: To save generated wallpapers on your device.
  • Media access (optional): To let you choose custom images from your gallery.
These permissions are strictly used to support app features and never to collect personal data.

4. Use of Open-Source Assets

WallpaperCreator uses anime artwork and graphic elements available under open licenses. We respect intellectual property rights and ensure that all assets used are permitted for redistribution and modification. 5. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be clearly communicated within the app and this page will reflect the new date.
