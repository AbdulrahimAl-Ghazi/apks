Privacy Policy for VideoGo

Effective Date: July 20, 2025

1. No Personal Data Collection

VideoGo does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users. The app does not require you to create an account or provide your name, email, phone number, or any other personal data.

2. Access to Local Media Files

The app requests access to video files stored on your device (READ_MEDIA_VIDEO) solely to allow you to browse and play your own local videos. We do not upload, share, or transmit these files. All video playback happens locally on your device.

3. No Internet Communication

VideoGo does not send or receive any user data over the internet. All functionality is performed entirely on the user’s device.

4. Children’s Privacy

The app is not intended for children and does not knowingly collect any personal data from anyone, including users under the age of 13.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed. Any changes will be posted on this page.
