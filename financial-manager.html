Privacy Policy for My Financial Manager

Last updated: August 14, 2025

We respect your privacy. My Financial Manager is a simple app for recording income and expenses. It does not collect, store, or share any personal information about you.

Data Collection

The app does not collect analytics, personal identifiers, location data, device information, or any other data.

Permissions

The app does not request permissions to access your device’s data, sensors, or the internet.

Local Storage Only

All information you enter remains stored locally on your device. No data is transmitted to our servers or to third parties.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because no data is collected.

Children’s Privacy

The app does not collect any data from users of any age.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, the updated version and its new “Last updated” date will be posted here.

This policy contains no contact information by design.
