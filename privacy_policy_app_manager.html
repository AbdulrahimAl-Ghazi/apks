1. Information We Collect

We collect certain types of information when you use the app, including:

  • Technical Information: The app may collect information about your device, such as device type, operating system, version, device identifiers (e.g., advertising ID), and other technical data.

2. How We Use the Information

We use the information collected to provide the app's core functionalities, including:

  • App Management: To let you manage installed apps, such as sharing, backing up, or uninstalling them.
  • Activity Viewing: To display app activities, with the option to open or search through them.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties. All information collected by the app remains on your device and is not transmitted to any external server.

  • Storage Access: To create backups of your apps.
  • Internet Access: To verify links and open apps on the Google Play Store.

5. Data Security

We take the security of your information seriously. All data collected by the app remains on your device. We use appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.
